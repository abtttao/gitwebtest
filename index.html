Hi LAY !!

Zhang Yixing (Chinese: 张艺兴; born October 7, 1991),[1] known professionally as Lay Zhang or simply Lay (Korean: 레이), is a Chinese singer-songwriter, record producer, dancer, and actor. He is a member of the South Korean-Chinese boy group Exo and its sub-group Exo-M under SM Entertainment.[2] He was first known after participating in the Chinese TV talent show Star Academy in 2005.[3] Apart from his musical activities, he is also known for starring in numerous dramas and movies such as Ex-Files 2 (2015), The Mystic Nine (2016), Kung Fu Yoga (2017) and The Island (2018).
